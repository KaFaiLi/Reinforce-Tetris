How to read this

Every step the agent sees 11 features (danger straight/right/left, its direction, and where the food is) and picks one of three relative actions: straight, turn right, turn left. Q (chosen) is the network's value for the action it took; the triple shows all three. Episodes end on collision or when the snake circles too long without eating (hunger limit).
